History

Hawk Air is an airplane company that was established in January, 2020. Ever since, we have been growing rapidly, so we decided to create a website for our company. We offer a lot of daily flights to many countries and cities. We are also partners in the Frequent Flyer program and we have a huge amount of customers. Many people trust our services!

Our Goals

Every year, nearly 2 million travelers trust Hawk Air to get them where they’re going. To us, travel is about more than just bringing people to a place. It’s about bringing people together.
